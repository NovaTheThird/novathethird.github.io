Developer Note

As one of three developers working on this project, SHUTTLEFALL is my first game
made at DigiPen: Institute of Technology. Made completely in C using the C Processing
framework, this is the culmination of 4 months of work. If you're willing to decipher
our module system, you can add your modules in the provided "modules.sfdat" file.
